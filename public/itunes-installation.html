iTunes Installation Instructions

Installing the profile

  1. Connect your iPad, Phone, or Pod touch to your computer and sync with iTunes.
  2. Download the provisioning profile by clicking the Download Profile button of the application you want to install.
  3. Mac: Drag-and-Drop the downloaded file onto the iTunes icon in the Dock.
  4. Windows: Drag-and-Drop the downloaded file into the Library section of the iTunes sidebar.
  5. If you are asked to replace a profile with the same name, go ahead and do so.
  6. Go to iTunes, select your device, and click Sync.
  7. Confirm that the provisioning profile is on your device. On the device, go to Home screen->Settings icon->General->Profiles (at the bottom of the page above Reset), and confirm you have the new profile in your Profiles list.

Installing the application

  1. Download the application by clicking the Download Application button of the application you want to install.
  2. Mac: Drag-and-Drop the downloaded file onto the iTunes icon in the Dock.
  3. Windows: Drag-and-Drop the downloaded file into the Library section of the iTunes sidebar.
  4. Go to iTunes, select your device, and click Sync.
  5. The application should now be installed on your device and visible on your home screen.
